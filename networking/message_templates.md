# Networking Message Templates

Copy, fill in the `[brackets]`, and adapt. Keep the tone warm and concise.

---

## Connection Requests (≤ 300 chars)

### Alumni angle
```
Hi [Name], I noticed we're both [Your University] alums! I'm a recent M.S. AI grad
exploring ML Engineer roles — would love to be in your network. No pressure to
respond beyond accepting!
```

### Shared research / technical interest
```
Hi [Name], I came across your work on [specific project / paper / post].
[One genuine observation]. I'm a grad ML researcher exploring roles at [company]
— would love to connect!
```

### Mutual connection
```
Hi [Name], [Mutual's name] suggested I reach out. I'm applying to [role] at
[company] and they thought we might have a lot to talk about. Would love to
connect!
```

### Recruiter / hiring manager (direct but polite)
```
Hi [Name], I'm exploring [role] opportunities at [company] — I think my
background in [ML / CUDA / LLM infra] could be a strong fit. Would love to
connect and learn more about the team!
```

## Follow-Up After Connecting (first message, 3–7 days after acceptance)

### Informational interview request
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for connecting! I'm currently wrapping up my M.S. in AI and exploring
ML Engineer roles. I came across your profile while looking at [company], and
I'd love to learn more about your experience there.

Would you be open to a 15-minute virtual chat? Totally flexible on timing — I
can work around your schedule.

Thanks again, and no worries if you're too busy right now!
[Your Name]
```

### Quick question (async, no meeting needed)
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for connecting! Quick question if you have a moment:

I'm applying for the [role] at [company] (Req ID: [id if known]). Given your
experience on [their team/area], is there anything about my background I should
make sure to highlight? I have an M.S. in AI with a focus on [relevant area].

No need for a call — even a sentence or two would be hugely helpful.
Thanks so much!
[Your Name]
```

## Referral Ask

> Use only after at least one genuine exchange. Never as a cold first message.

```
Hi [Name],

Really appreciated [our conversation / your insights on X]. I've been preparing
my application for [role] at [company] and I wanted to ask — if you feel it
would be a good fit, would you be comfortable submitting a referral?

Here's a quick summary to make it easy to forward if needed:

---
[Your Name] | M.S. AI, [Your University]
Background: ML engineering, CUDA optimization, PyTorch, LLM pipelines
LinkedIn: [your LinkedIn URL]
Applying for: [Role] | Req ID: [id if known]
---

Absolutely no pressure — I understand completely if it's not the right moment!
Thanks either way.
[Your Name]
```

## Thank-You After Informational Chat

Send within 24h.

```
Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to chat today — I really appreciate it.
[One specific thing they said that was valuable].

I'll be [applying / following up on my application] this week with your advice
in mind. I'll keep you posted on how it goes.

Thanks again, and I hope our paths cross again!
[Your Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up on Application (2+ weeks, no response)

Send to recruiter or HR contact, not the engineer you networked with.

```
Subject: Following up — [Role] Application | [Your Name]

Hi [Recruiter Name],

I hope you're doing well. I'm writing to follow up on my application for the
[Role] position (Req ID: [id]) submitted on [date]. I'm very excited about this
opportunity and wanted to confirm my application is under consideration.

Please let me know if you need any additional materials. I'm happy to provide
references or a work sample at your convenience.

Thank you for your time!

Best,
[Your Name]
you@example.com | +1 (555) 123-4567
LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/yourprofile
```

## Declining an Offer (gracefully)

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you so much for extending the offer for the [Role] position at [Company].
I greatly enjoyed the interview process and was truly impressed by [specific
positive aspect of company/team].

After careful consideration, I have decided to accept another offer that more
closely aligns with my current career goals. This was a genuinely difficult
decision.

I have a lot of respect for the work being done at [Company] and hope our paths
cross again in the future.

Thank you again for this opportunity.

Best,
[Your Name]
```
